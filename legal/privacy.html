Privacy Policy

プライバシーポリシー

English 日本語

TechMirai AI ("we", "us", "our") operates techmirai-ai.com. This policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and the rights you have over it. We comply with Japan's Act on the Protection of Personal Information (個人情報保護法, APPI).

1.Information we collect

  • Information you provide: name, company, email, phone number, industry, and the content of any message you submit through our contact form or by direct email.
  • Automatically collected: IP address, browser type, device type, referring URL, pages visited, and timestamps. We use this only to operate, secure, and improve the site.
  • Cookies: we use minimal first-party cookies to remember your language preference. We do not use third-party advertising or behavioral tracking cookies.

2.How we use information

  • To respond to inquiries and provide the services you requested.
  • To send transactional email (e.g. follow-ups to a strategy audit you booked).
  • To prevent abuse, fraud, and security incidents.
  • To comply with legal obligations under Japanese law.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it to train third-party AI models.

3.Sharing

We share personal information only with:

  • Service providers strictly necessary to operate the site and deliver our services (e.g. our email/SMTP provider, our hosting provider). These parties are bound by contractual confidentiality and data-processing terms.
  • Authorities when required by law (court order, regulatory request).

For client engagements, customer data we process on behalf of a client (e.g. conversation logs from a deployed AI agent) is held in isolated, client-specific environments and is never commingled with other clients' data.

4.Retention

Inquiry data is retained for up to 24 months, after which it is deleted unless an active business relationship exists. Server access logs are retained for up to 90 days.

5.Security

We use industry-standard safeguards including TLS encryption in transit, encryption at rest for sensitive data, role-based access controls, and audit logging. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to minimize risk.

6.International transfers

TechMirai AI operates from Japan with engineering staff in Bangladesh. Personal data may be processed in either country. Where data is transferred internationally, we apply equivalent safeguards consistent with APPI guidelines.

7.Your rights

Under APPI you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion or suspension of use.
  • Withdraw consent for optional processing at any time.

To exercise these rights, email info@techmirai-ai.com. We respond within 30 days.

8.Children

Our services are not directed to individuals under 16, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

9.Changes

We may update this policy. The "last updated" date below reflects the latest revision. Material changes will be communicated by a notice on this page.
